Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable gaming experience for all, please adhere to the following rules.

General Rules

Community Rules

  • Respectful interaction with all players is mandatory
  • No insults or discrimination
  • No advertising for other servers
  • No cheats or modifications
  • No metagaming or powergaming

Vehicle Rules

Vehicle Behavior

  • Realistic driving is mandatory
  • No vehicle spawns in the city
  • Vehicles must be insured
  • No vehicle modifications while driving
  • Speed limits must be observed
  • Vehicles must be properly parked

Roleplay Rules

Roleplay Principles

  • Realistic roleplay is mandatory
  • No OOC communication in-game
  • Characters must be realistic
  • No random deathmatches
  • Roleplay situations must be played to the end
  • No teleportation without RP reason

Penalty System

First Violation

Warning and documentation

Second Violation

Temporary ban (24h)

Third Violation

Temporary ban (7 days)

Serious Violations

Permanent ban
