Frequently Asked Questions

Server Join

How do I join the server?
To join the server, you need FiveM and must register in our Discord server. Then follow the instructions in the #server-join channel. After registration, you will receive your access credentials via Discord message.
What are the requirements?
You need a legal copy of GTA V, FiveM, and a microphone for voice chat communication. A Discord account is also required. Make sure your computer meets the minimum requirements.

Economy & Jobs

How does the currency system work?
Our server uses a realistic currency system. Money can be earned through legal and illegal activities. All transactions are logged. You can deposit money in the bank and earn interest.
What jobs are available?
We offer a variety of jobs including police officer, paramedic, mechanic, taxi driver, and many more. Each job has its own requirements and benefits. Check the #jobs channel on Discord for more information.

Vehicles & Properties

How can I buy a vehicle?
Vehicles can be purchased at car dealerships in the city. You need sufficient money and a valid driver's license. After purchase, you must insure and register the vehicle.
How does the property system work?
You can buy or rent houses and apartments in the city. Each property can be furnished and used as storage. Prices vary by location and size.

Rules & Penalties

What happens if I break the rules?
Rule violations are punished according to severity. From warnings to temporary or permanent bans. All penalties are documented in the #penalties channel. Contact the support team if you have questions.
How can I appeal a ban?
If you believe a ban was issued unjustly, you can submit an appeal in the #ban-appeals channel. Please make sure to provide all relevant information.
